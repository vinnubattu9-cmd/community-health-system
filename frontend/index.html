Core Objectives

A systematic framework dedicated to improving local public health oversight, medical awareness, and wellness accessibility.

Health Monitoring

Enables individuals to log critical health parameters (BP, SpO2, glucose, temperature) to keep a clear record of personal health history.

Health Analysis

Runs intelligent client and server-side logic to assess vital stability, calculate BMI categories, and flag potential risk areas instantly.

Report Generation

Creates detailed, structured medical reports containing recommendations, ready for sharing, printing, or offline A4 PDF downloading.

Community Awareness

Aggregates survey insights anonymously into central charts to guide policy decisions, medical camps, and wellness initiatives.
